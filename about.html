About Me

Hello, I'm Chandru M, Pursing MSc MicroBiology in RVS College of Arts and Science And i am passionate individual with a strong background in BioTechnology.

This website techniques like Front-end Development to analyze genomes and forecast Rhizosphere molecular pathways in order to locate growth-promoting microorganisms and diseases caused by microorganisms in plants. For the experiment, a published research paper data set was used. Front-end Development is one of the machine learning approaches employed in this work. Exploring these microbes by unraveling their positive and negative interactions with plants.
